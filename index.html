About Our Natural Farming

Traditional Methods, Modern Results

At Chittoor Natural Farms, we believe in working with nature, not against it. Our farming practices are rooted in traditional knowledge passed down through generations, combined with sustainable modern techniques.

Located in the fertile lands of Chittoor district, our farms benefit from rich soil and favorable climate conditions that allow us to grow a wide variety of vegetables year-round without the use of harmful chemicals or pesticides.

We are committed to preserving the environment while providing our community with nutritious, flavorful produce that supports local health and economy.

Our Fresh Vegetables

Tomatoes

Juicy, flavorful tomatoes grown naturally without chemical fertilizers. Rich in vitamins and antioxidants.

Brinjal

Fresh, purple brinjals with excellent texture and taste. Perfect for traditional Andhra recipes.

Okra (Bhendi)

Tender, green okra with minimal fiber. Grown using organic compost and natural pest control.

Chillies

Spicy green chillies with perfect heat levels. Essential for Andhra cuisine and packed with vitamin C.

Beans

Fresh string beans, crisp and nutritious. Grown on natural trellises without chemical inputs.

Cucumber

Cool, hydrating cucumbers perfect for salads and raitas. Grown with natural water conservation methods.

Carrots

Sweet, crunchy carrots rich in beta-carotene. Grown in deep, well-drained natural soil.

Radish

Peppery, crisp radishes perfect for salads and traditional dishes. Grown using natural mulching techniques.
